Master's Thesis: Development of a Mobile Educational Application for Enchancing Congitive and Language Skills of Children with Disabilities.

Master Thesis Description: The application designed for Android on Android Studio and contains 18 activities. There are different kinds of activities like drag and drop, choosing, writing and vocal pronunciation where each activity trains a particular skill. The application includes user authentication with funtions like Log In, Register, Change Password etc. Every child has an account and he's/her's progress is been saved on an online database (Google Firebase). The child's instructor inspects child's progress, in a specific progress tab, and he chooses which skill needs to be trained each time.

Publications

A Mobile Educational Application for Enchancing Cognitive and Language Skills of Children with Disabilities

Springer Link - Apr 9, 2022

The current paper aims at the development of a mobile educational application for enhancing cognitive and language skills of children with disabilities. During their development, they receive a variety of stimuli that influence and shape these skills. Therefore, they should be helped and supported as much as possible with all available means. Additionally, the identification of language and cognitive disorders is a major challenge to special education teachers. It is thus important to develop a specially designed application aiming at the enhancement of skills of children with disabilities and will be available to all stakeholders (special education teachers, children, parents).
